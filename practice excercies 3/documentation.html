01 Introduction

IWantPC is a Philippine-based online PC hardware store website developed to provide Filipino consumers with a centralized, trustworthy, and locally-focused platform for purchasing computer components, peripherals, and booking repair services. The platform was built in response to the growing demand for an accessible e-commerce solution that features transparent peso-based pricing, real customer reviews, and integrated after-sales support — all in a single, cohesive web experience.

While international PC hardware platforms exist, they often lack local context, Philippine Peso (₱) pricing, and localized repair support services. Filipino consumers and PC builders are left navigating fragmented channels — social media pages, marketplaces, and walk-in stores — with no single reliable digital storefront that combines product browsing, cart management, order tracking, customer reviews, and repair scheduling. IWantPC addresses this gap by bringing all these capabilities together under one modern, responsive, and user-friendly website.

The IWantPC website is built using HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript for the frontend, with a Node.js (Express) backend and a PostgreSQL relational database. The design follows a clean, professional blue color scheme that reflects trust, precision, and technology — values aligned with the expectations of the Philippine PC hardware market.

Website Features

Homepage: A visually engaging landing page with a hero banner, category shortcuts, featured products, and a customer reviews section.
Products Page: A full catalog with category filter, keyword search, sort options, and stock indicators on every card.
Product Detail Page: Full product information — image, description, price in ₱, stock count, Add to Cart, and a customer review section with star ratings.
Shopping Cart & Checkout: Cart management with quantity controls, order summary, and checkout with email confirmation.
Order Tracking: View order history, track status (Pending → Processing → Completed), and submit refund requests on completed orders.
Repair Booking: Users book repair consultations by submitting device details and preferred schedule through an online form.
Login & Registration: Secure user authentication with role-based access — customers and administrators have separate views and permissions.
Admin Dashboard: Full product management (add/edit/delete/upload images), order and repair booking management, refund handling, and site statistics.
Customer Reviews: Logged-in users can rate and review products with a 1–5 star interactive rating form.
Responsive Design: The site adapts to all screen sizes — desktop, tablet, and mobile — ensuring a consistent experience on any device.

01 Introduction (continued)

Website Objectives

  • To develop a fully functional Philippine PC hardware e-commerce website with product management, cart, checkout, and order tracking capabilities.
  • To provide Filipino consumers with a centralized, locally-contextualized platform for browsing and purchasing PC components at transparent ₱ pricing.
  • To enable customers to book repair consultations and track their service requests online, reducing friction in after-sales support.
  • To allow customers to leave honest product reviews, building trust and informed purchasing decisions within the community.
  • To give administrators a complete dashboard for managing inventory, orders, repair requests, and customer refund submissions efficiently.

Problem Questions

Problems
How do Filipino PC consumers currently struggle with online hardware purchasing given the lack of a locally-focused, peso-priced, and review-verified Philippine PC hardware store?
Questions
What aspects need to be considered when developing the IWantPC website to ensure it benefits both customers and administrators in terms of usability, functionality, and reliability?
Solution
IWantPC provides a fully local, peso-priced, review-supported e-commerce platform with integrated repair booking and order management — all accessible through a clean, mobile-friendly web interface.
Benefits
Customers gain a trustworthy platform to browse and purchase PC components confidently. Administrators gain full inventory and order management tools, improving operational visibility and customer satisfaction.
